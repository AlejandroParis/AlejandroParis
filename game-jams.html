COVID-19: Historias Compartidas

COVID-19: Historias Compartidas is a narrative point and click game.

Game in which you will have to overcome several days of alertness impersonating both our protagonist Carmen who will spend her days at home and Julio, the titular nurse during this difficult situation. As the days go by, we will discover what the future will hold and whether by being responsible citizens they will avoid the worst possible results.

Emote Me!

Emote Me! is a small cooperative puzzle game

Game in which a pair of robots must solve puzzles in order to escape from their suffering.

Game in which you can only communicate with your partner with a set of 4 emotes through which you will have to make yourselves understood to solve the puzzles and complete the levels in a cooperative way.
